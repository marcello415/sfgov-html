The Administration at Mid-Mark

 

We are pleased that you have chosen this Website to learn more about our city government and how various agencies, departments and committees are constantly working to better serve the needs of the public and the San Francisco community.

 

On January 8, 2006, we marked the mid-way point of my administration. Sworn in two years ago with a pledge to find common ground and a promise to advance real solutions to the challenges facing our city, we have made great progress- and we have much work left to do.

 

The City & County of San Francisco welcomes your comments and encourages you to get involved with our collective efforts to improve the way local government does business.

 

Thank you for taking the time to visit our Website!

Gavin Newsom
Mayor
